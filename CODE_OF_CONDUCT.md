# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project represents a place of faith, tradition, and community service.
All contributors are expected to behave with **respect, integrity, and responsibility**.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone involved in this project.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants must:

- Treat others with dignity and respect
- Use inclusive, polite, and professional language
- Be mindful that this project has **religious and cultural significance**
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus discussions on improving the project, not individuals
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is **not tolerated**:

- Disrespectful, abusive, or insulting language
- Religious, caste-based, political, or ideological debates
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Mockery of religious beliefs, rituals, or traditions
- Publishing false, misleading, or inappropriate content
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Pushing personal agendas unrelated to the project

Any such behavior may result in removal from the project without warning.

---

## Content Responsibility

Contributors must ensure that:

- Religious text, names, and terminology are accurate
- Content is culturally sensitive and appropriate
- Images and media are respectful and relevant
- No copyrighted material is added without permission
- No misinformation is introduced knowingly or unknowingly

If unsure about content accuracy, **do not guess** — ask first.

---

## Scope Discipline

This is a **static website project**.

Do not:
- Introduce backend services
- Add frameworks or heavy dependencies
- Change the core design philosophy
- Over-engineer solutions

All changes should be minimal, intentional, and maintainable.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include:
- Requesting changes
- Removing content
- Closing issues or pull requests
- Temporary or permanent contributor ban

Decisions by maintainers are final.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior:

- Open a **private issue**, or
- Contact the repository maintainer directly

All reports will be reviewed discreetly and respectfully.

---

## Our Shared Responsibility

This project exists for devotees, visitors, and the wider community.
Every contribution should **add value**, not noise.

Let’s work together with discipline, humility, and devotion.
