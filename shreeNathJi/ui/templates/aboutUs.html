मारी हवेली

ShreeNathJi Haweli, located in Ahilyanagr, Maharashtra, is a revered place of worship dedicated to Lord ShreeNathJi. Established with the aim of promoting spiritual growth and community service, the Haweli serves as a hub for devotees to gather, pray, and participate in various religious and cultural activities. The architecture of the Haweli reflects traditional Indian design, featuring intricate carvings and vibrant colors that create a serene and sacred atmosphere. The Haweli also hosts regular events, including devotional singing, festivals, and charitable initiatives aimed at supporting the local community. Visitors to ShreeNathJi Haweli can expect a warm welcome and an enriching spiritual experience.
